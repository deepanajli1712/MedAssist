Home Remedies

Cold & Cough

  • Ginger-Honey Tea: Boil ginger in water, strain, and add honey.
  • Turmeric Milk (Golden Milk): Warm milk with turmeric powder helps reduce inflammation.
  • Steam Inhalation: Hot water + a pinch of salt or eucalyptus oil.

Headache

  • Apply a cold pack to your forehead.
  • Peppermint or Eucalyptus Oil on Temples.
  • Hydration with Lemon Water and avoid skipping meals.
Reference Video

Indigestion

  • Ajwain (carom seeds) + Salt: Chew to relieve gas.
  • Buttermilk with Black Salt: Aids digestion and reduces acidity.
  • Ginger-Lemon Water: Boosts digestion and soothes stomach
Reference Video

Minor Burns

  • Cool the burn under running water.
  • Apply aloe vera gel.
  • Do not apply ice directly.
Reference Video

Stress & Sleep

  • Chamomile or Tulsi Tea: Natural relaxant.
  • Warm Foot Soak: Salt + warm water reduces fatigue.
  • Oil Massage (Head or Feet): Coconut or mustard oil before bedtime.
Reference Video

Acne Issues

  • Multani Mitti (Fuller's Earth) Mask: Removes excess oil and cools skin.
  • Crush neem leaves into a paste; apply on affected areas.
  • Aloe Vera Gel: For burns, acne, or dryness.
Reference Video

Toothache / Oral Health

  • Clove Oil Dab: On affected tooth.
  • Salt Water Rinse: Reduces swelling and kills bacteria.
  • Neem Twig Chewing: Natural toothbrush substitute.
Reference Video

Immunity Boosters

  • Chyawanprash (Ayurvedic Jam)
  • Kadha (Herbal Decoction): Tulsi, ginger, cinnamon, black pepper boiled together.
  • Lemon & Honey in Warm Water (Empty Stomach)

Body Pain & Joint Pain

  • Turmeric Milk (Haldi Doodh)
